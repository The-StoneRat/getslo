Part II: Privacy and Data Retention Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

Slo camera acts as the Data Fiduciary under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDP Act"). We are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("Data Principals") by enforcing an optimal equilibrium between service availability and data minimization principles. This document outlines exactly how personal identifiers and digital imagery are handled, processed, stored, and systematically erased.

1. Data Items Collected and Purpose of Processing

Slo camera collects the minimum necessary digital personal data required to operate cross-border e-commerce operations. Under the principle of purpose limitation, data elements are processed across these vectors:

  • Identity & Profile Elements: Full Name, Email Address, and Mobile Contact Number. Purpose: To register user accounts, verify identity, and broadcast tracking updates.
  • Logistics & Shipping Data: Structured Physical Address, Postal/ZIP code, City, and Country. Purpose: To transmit physical print files to localized delivery couriers and clear international custom checkpoints inside the 7-8 business days turnaround.
  • Image Content Data: Digital photographs captured within the two supported sizes through the Slo camera system. Purpose: To render templates, compile production sheets, and physically output your images onto print stock.

2. Photo Privacy & Structured Retention Architecture

In accordance with Section 8(7) of the DPDP Act, 2023, data items must not be retained past the completion of the target business purpose. Slo camera applies a strict, automated data hygiene cycle governed by the following timelines:

  • Captured but Unused Images: Digital photos captured within the app that are not associated with a saved layout template or checkout cart are retained for a maximum of 90 days to ensure session recovery, after which they are completely scrubbed.
  • Saved Layout Designs (No Order Placed): Photographs utilized inside a persistently saved user layout draft are maintained for up to 12 months to allow future check-outs.
  • Completed Print Orders: Photographs that have successfully completed check-out, print production, and billing are maintained for up to 24 months following shipment. This extended window protects the user by enabling seamless re-orders, and resolving delivery disputes or print-quality appeals. Following this period, they are automatically and permanently purged from our storage arrays.
  • Guest User Sessions: Image content processed by unauthenticated guest users is kept for an abbreviated timeline of 30 to 90 days.

Reset Parameter: If any image asset is proactively reused in an entirely new print configuration or supplementary checkout stream prior to its expiration date, its specific retention countdown resets from that event forward.

3. Rights of the Data Principal

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, you maintain comprehensive legal authority over your data. You may execute these rights through our compliance desk:

  • Right to Summary & Access: Request a complete summary of personal metrics currently processed by Slo camera and see the list of third-party logistics handlers executing delivery.
  • Right to Correction & Deletion: Correct typo entries in your physical shipping records or order immediate premature erasure of your account drafts and associated photographs.
  • Right to Consent Withdrawal: You may retract your permission settings at any moment. Note that withdrawing address tracking privileges while an order is in active dispatch will disrupt fulfillment.

4. Children's Data Governance

Slo camera does not knowingly process digital personal data from individuals under 18 years of age without verifiable parental or guardian consent. We completely bar tracking user behaviors or pushing targeted ads toward minors inside our platform ecosystem in accordance with Section 9 of the DPDP Act.

5. Data Breach Notification & Framework Safeguards

We maintain full technical safeguards, including end-to-end transport encryption, isolated cloud database instances, and rigid role-based processing privileges. In the unforeseen event of a data breach, Slo camera will immediately issue clear statutory notifications to the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI) and all affected Data Principals.
