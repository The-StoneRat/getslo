Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Slo ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Slo (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account or sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect your email address, name, username, and profile information provided by Google.
  • Contact Information: We may collect your phone number and address information when you complete your profile or place print orders.
  • User Content: We collect photos and images that you capture using the App's camera or select from your device's gallery for the purpose of creating print orders.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, time spent in the App, and crash reports.
  • Camera and Gallery Access: When you grant permission, we access your device's camera to capture photos and your gallery to select existing photos for print orders.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: When you authenticate using Google Sign-In, we receive your Google account information (email, name, profile picture) as permitted by your Google account settings.
  • Analytics and Crash Reporting: We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to collect anonymized usage statistics and crash reports to improve App performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve the App's core functionality, including photo capture, print order creation, and marketplace features.
  • Authentication: To authenticate your identity and manage your account.
  • Order Processing: To process and fulfill your print orders, including storing and transmitting your photos to our print service providers. Photos are automatically deleted from our servers after your print order has been completed and fulfilled.
  • Communication: To send you notifications about order status, App updates, and important service-related communications.
  • Analytics: To analyze App usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and improve user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Your account information, photos, and print orders are stored on Supabase cloud infrastructure, which uses industry-standard security measures. Photos uploaded for print orders are stored temporarily and are automatically deleted from our servers once the print order has been completed and fulfilled.
  • Local Storage: Photos selected for print orders are temporarily stored on your device in encrypted app-specific directories until upload is complete.
  • Offline Storage: The App uses local Room database storage to enable offline functionality and improve performance.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase: We use Supabase for backend services, database storage, authentication, and cloud storage. Supabase processes your data in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics, which are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Print Service Providers: We share your photos and order information with third-party print service providers to fulfill your print orders. Once your print order is completed, photos are deleted from our servers.

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or comply with a legal process.

5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Permissions and Device Access

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Camera Permission: Required to capture photos using your device's camera.
  • Storage Permission: Required to access photos from your device's gallery (Android 12 and below). On Android 13 and above, we use the system photo picker which does not require this permission.
  • Notification Permission: Required to send you notifications about order status and App updates.
  • Internet Permission: Required for App functionality, including authentication, data synchronization, and order processing.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, though this may limit certain App functionality.

7. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For user authentication. Subject to Google Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: For backend infrastructure, database, and storage. Subject to Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Google): For analytics and crash reporting. Subject to Google Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

8. Data Retention

  • Account Information: We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.
  • Photos: Photos uploaded for print orders are retained on our servers only until your print order has been completed and fulfilled. Once your print order is completed, photos are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers. We may retain order metadata (such as order number, date, and status) for order history and customer support purposes, but the actual photo files are deleted.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely.
  • Local Data: Photos stored locally on your device are automatically deleted after successful upload or when you uninstall the App.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements. Note that photos are automatically deleted from our servers after print order completion.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of certain data collection, such as analytics, through your device settings.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 12.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
